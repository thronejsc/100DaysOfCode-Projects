The Best Movies according to Tracy

My Top 3 Movies of all time

Forrest Gump

Forrest Gump is a movie about a man with a simple mind who unwittingly influences major historical events.

The Cat Returns

The Cat Returns is a charming animated film where a high school girl saves a cat and finds herself pulled into a whimsical feline world, leading to unexpected adventures.

It Could Happen to You

It Could Happen to You is a romantic comedy where a kindhearted cop's life takes a surprising turn when he splits his lottery ticket with a waitress, sparking a series of unpredictable and heartwarming events.
